# The Agentic PM

**Product Management in the Age of AI** — An interactive presentation by [Asaf Nakash](https://www.linkedin.com/in/asafnakash/).

## [→ View the Presentation](https://nakashon.github.io/the-agentic-pm/)

## What's Inside

A 24-slide interactive deck exploring how AI is transforming the product management role — from foundations to the future.

### Part 1 — The Foundations
What product managers actually do: the role, the product lifecycle, MVP thinking, and feedback loops.

### Part 2 — The Shift
What changed with AI: the evolution from traditional PM to Agentic PM, new workflows, new skills, and the framework for thriving in this new reality.

### The Agentic PM Framework
> **Orchestrate → Review → Decide → Ship → Learn**

## Key Takeaway

> *"The future of PM isn't AI vs. humans. It's humans who know how to use AI vs. humans who don't."*

## Usage

Open `index.html` in any browser — no build step, no dependencies, no setup.

Use it, remix it, present it to your team.

Navigate with arrow keys. Press `Esc` for slide overview.

## Tech

Single-file [reveal.js](https://revealjs.com/) presentation. All dependencies loaded via CDN.
